ABOUT

Our Company

We are Addis Life Real Estate PLC a Luxury residential and commercial properties developer with our Head office located in Addis Ababa. Our company has quickly made a name for itself in the real estate market and has established strong relationships with various clients and partners. Even though established in 2019 GC we have long years of experience in the construction industry with our sister company Ruh International Trading PLC which has a manufacturing plant in Sululta for the production of construction chemicals and building materials.

Our company's main objective is to provide luxury residential apartments and commercial spaces for sales as well as for rent. We have experienced huge success and growth in the past years and have expanded our portfolio to include a variety of properties in desirable locations in the heart of Addis Ababa, right in front of African Union, around Canada Embassy and Blgaria. We consistently receive positive feedback from our clients which inspires our team to work tirelessly to provide exceptional living experiences, constantly seeking out new ways to improve our properties and services.

We are proud of our achievements over the past years. As we look to the future, we are excited to continue providing outstanding services to our existing and future clients, and to build upon the success that we have achieved thus far.
